our story

Coffee House is a part of Coffee Day Globle Limited,is India's favourite hangout for coffee and conversations.We strive to provide the best experience to our guests.Our coffees are sourced from thousands of small coffee planters,who made us who we are today and we are glad to be a part of their lives.We opened our first cafe in 1997 at Una in Himachal pradesh.And now Coffee House is present in almost every state and we are glad to serve you our delicious coffee.

What we do

Coffee House to the youth is a “hangout” spot where they meet people, make conversations, and have a whole lot of fun over steaming cups of great coffee.It's been an exciting journey since then to becoming the largest organised retail cafe chain in the country. We’re also present in Austria, Czech Republic and Malaysia, so if your travel takes you there, do stop by our outlets to get a taste from back home!
